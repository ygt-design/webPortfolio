About Factory Reset


Factory Reset, is a collaborative online exhibition that has been put up by graphic design students. In order to create a more original and creative environment, designers rethink the concept of graphic design. Old PC setup UIs served as the project's primary source of inspiration. By utilising basic and contrasting design concepts, it updates the designs and elements from the previous computer era. It is a test project for branding.
